StartupXYZ came to us as a fledgling company in the fintech space, needing a brand identity that would convey trust, innovation, and approachability.

We developed a comprehensive brand identity system that would scale with their growth across digital and print mediums.

### Deliverables

- **Logo Design** — Primary, secondary, and icon variations
- **Color Palette** — Carefully selected colors with accessibility in mind
- **Typography** — Custom type scale and font pairing recommendations
- **Brand Guidelines** — Comprehensive documentation for internal and external use

### The Process

Through a series of collaborative workshops, we identified the brand's core values and visual direction. Multiple concepts were presented and refined until we arrived at a final identity that resonated with both the founders and their target audience.
