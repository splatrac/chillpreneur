TechCorp needed a modern e-commerce solution that could handle their growing catalog of over 10,000 products while providing a seamless shopping experience for their customers.

We built a custom platform from the ground up using a headless architecture, allowing for maximum flexibility and scalability. The front end was crafted with a focus on conversion optimization, fast load times, and intuitive navigation.

### Key Features

- **Custom Dashboard** — Real-time analytics, inventory management, and order tracking
- **Payment Integration** — Multi-gateway support including Stripe, PayPal, and regional providers
- **Smart Search** — AI-powered product search with faceted filtering
- **Responsive Design** — Optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop experiences

### Results

The new platform saw a 40% increase in conversion rate within the first three months and reduced page load times by 60%.
