LearnOnline aimed to democratize education by building an accessible online learning platform that could scale to serve thousands of students worldwide.

We created a robust learning management system that prioritizes student engagement and provides educators with powerful tools to create and manage their courses.

### Key Features

- **Video Courses** — Structured learning paths with progress tracking
- **Quiz System** — Automated grading with instant feedback
- **Student Dashboard** — Personalized learning recommendations and achievements
- **Instructor Tools** — Course creation suite with analytics

### Impact

The platform now hosts over 500 courses and serves more than 100,000 active students, with a course completion rate that exceeds industry averages by 25%.
