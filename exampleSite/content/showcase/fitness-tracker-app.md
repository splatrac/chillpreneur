HealthTech wanted to bring a comprehensive fitness companion to market that would help users stay consistent with their health goals through intelligent tracking and personalized recommendations.

We developed a cross-platform mobile application using React Native, ensuring a native feel on both iOS and Android while maintaining a single codebase.

### Key Features

- **Workout Tracking** — Custom exercise library with video demonstrations
- **Meal Planning** — Calorie tracking and macro-nutrient breakdown
- **Progress Monitoring** — Visual charts and streak tracking for motivation
- **Social Features** — Friend challenges and community leaderboards

### Impact

The app achieved over 50,000 downloads in its first month and maintained a 4.7-star rating across both app stores.
