Green Bistro, a beloved farm-to-table restaurant chain with five locations, needed a brand refresh that would reflect their commitment to sustainability while appealing to a modern audience.

We took a holistic approach, touching every customer touchpoint from the storefront signage to the digital presence.

### Deliverables

- **Logo & Wordmark** — Organic, earthy design with a modern twist
- **Menu Design** — Seasonal menu templates with beautiful food photography
- **Signage** — Interior and exterior signage for all five locations
- **Website** — A mobile-first website with online reservations and menu browsing

### Results

Customer engagement increased by 35% across digital channels, and the new branding was featured in several design publications.
